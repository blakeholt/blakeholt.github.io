Pepperoni Pizza

Servings: 1 per slice

Calories: 313 per slice

Prep Time: ~15 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • Frozen Pizza.

Instructions:

  1. Remove frozen pizza from the box.
  2. Follow the instructions on the box to cook the pizza in the oven.
  3. Once the specified time has elapsed, remove the pizza from the oven using oven mitts, and place it on a cutting board.
  4. Cut the pizza into 4, 6, or 8 slices.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
