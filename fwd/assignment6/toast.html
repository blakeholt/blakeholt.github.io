Toast

Servings: 1 Person

Calories: 313 per 100g

Prep Time: ~2 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • Bread.
  • Butter.

Instructions:

  1. Insert bread into toaster.
  2. Move dial on toaster to desired position.
  3. Pull down the lever on toaster.
  4. Await toaster to pop.
  5. Apply butter to toast.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
