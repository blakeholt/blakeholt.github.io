Soup

Servings: 1 Person

Calories: 170

Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Ingredients:

  • Can of Tomato Soup
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Open can of Tomato Soup.
  2. Put soup into bowl.
  3. Add water to the bowl.
  4. Mix.
  5. Microwave for ~3 minutes.
  6. Let cool, then serve and enjoy.
