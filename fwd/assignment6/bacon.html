Bacon

Servings: 1 person per ~3 slices

Calories: 54 per slice

Prep Time: xxx Minutes

Ingredients:

  • Raw Bacon

Instructions:

  1. Line sheetpan with foil.
  2. Evenly space the bacon on the sheetpan.
  3. Bake at ~191°C for 15 minutes.
  4. Flip bacon, then cook until finished, which should be about 5 minutes.
  5. Transfer bacon to paper towel lined bowl/plate to drain excess grease.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
